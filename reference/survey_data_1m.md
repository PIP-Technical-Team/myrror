# Survey Data 1:m Variation 1

Survey Data 1:m Variation 1

## Usage

``` r
survey_data_1m
```

## Format

A `data.table` with 36 rows and 6 variables:

- country:

  Factor with levels: "A", "B".

- year:

  Numeric, with values: 2010-2017.

- variable1:

  Numeric.

- variable2:

  Numeric.

- variable3:

  Numeric.

- variable4:

  Numeric.

## Source

Variation of `survey_data` with non-unique ids and a 1:m relationship
between ids and values.
